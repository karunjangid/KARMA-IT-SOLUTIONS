Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancellation Policy:
  • Customers can cancel their orders within 24 hours of placing them.
  • To cancel an order, customers must contact our customer service via email or phone.
  • Orders that have already been shipped cannot be canceled.
Refund Policy:
  • Refunds will be processed for canceled orders that meet the cancellation criteria.
  • If a product is defective or not as described, customers can request a refund within 7 days of receiving the item.
  • To initiate a refund, customers must provide proof of purchase and a description of the issue.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after approval.
Exceptions:
  • Certain items may be non-refundable, including personalized or custom-made products.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part.

Shipping and Delivery Policy

Shipping Methods:
  • We offer various shipping options, including standard and expedited shipping.
  • Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on the selected method and destination.
Delivery Time:
  • Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited shipping usually takes 2-3 business days.
  • Delivery times may vary based on location and availability.
Order Tracking:
  • Customers will receive a tracking number via email once their order has shipped.
  • Orders can be tracked through our website or the shipping carrier's website.
International Shipping:
  • We offer international shipping to select countries.
  • Customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes that may apply.
Shipping Issues:
  • If an order is lost or damaged during shipping, customers should contact our customer service for assistance.
  • We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue promptly.
